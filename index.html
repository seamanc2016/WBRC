About Our Community

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.” - Mahatma Gandhi

West Boca Retirement Community (WBRC) is a magnificent Active Adult +55 community that was built by Olman Luxury Suites from 2018 to 2022 and offered two different home collections; The Renaissance Collection and The Reserve Collection. Between both collections there are 500 single family homes in WBRC, which are situated on a waterfront or private lot.

The Renaissance Collection had 4 different floorplans to choose from with a living square footage ranging from 1,886 up to 2,374. The smallest home features 2 Bedrooms , 2 Bathrooms and a 2 Car Garage and the largest home also has 2 Bedrooms , but has a half bath more. Every home in this collection has a minimum lot size of 50'x110' and is one story.

The Reserve Collection had 3 different floorplans to choose from with a living square footage ranging from 2,535 up to 3,328. The smallest home features 3 Bedrooms , 3 Bathrooms and a 2 Car Garage while the largest home has 3 Bedrooms t and 3.5 Bathrooms. Lot sizes in the Reserve Collection are much larger than the Renaissance Collection and will be a minimum of 70'x110'.
